Terms & Conditions

Thank you for joining Hustle Platform as a Mentor! 🚀 Your guidance helps aspiring professionals reach their goals. Please review and accept our terms before proceeding.

Mentorship & Responsibilities

  • ✔️ You must have mentorship experience and a passion for helping participants.
  • ✔️ You must be fluent in at least two of the following languages: Kirundi, French, English, Swahili.
  • ✔️ You must review job applications before submission.

Reporting & Accountability

  • ✔️ You must provide weekly reports to the Platform Manager via email.
  • ✔️ You must communicate effectively and ensure mentee progress.

Job Application Submission

  • ✔️ Once you approve an application, your email will be shared for direct communication.
  • ✔️ You must professionally submit job applications and ensure the best results for participants.

Compensation & Ethics

  • ✔️ You will receive payment from the platform service fee.
  • ✔️ Helping comes before earning—this platform is for those who genuinely want to assist.
  • ✔️ All mentors must act ethically and fairly in mentorship and application processes.

Professionalism & Conduct

  • ✔️ Always be polite and professional in conversations with participants.
  • ✔️ Keep discussions instructive and goal-oriented.

By signing up, you agree to these terms.

Your mentorship has the power to change lives. Thank you for being part of this journey! 💙
